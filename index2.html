INGREDIENTS

  • 3 packs of chocolatos italia chocolate
  • agar-agar powder
  • 40gr of sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • -600ml of mineral water
  • 3 slices of bread

TOOLS

  • mixer
  • stove
  • pudding mold
  • spoon
  • pan
  • tray
  • bowl

STEPS

  1. cut the bread into a pieces,put bread,3 packs of chocolatos Italian chocolate,agar-agar powder,40gr of sugar,600ml of mineral water into the bowl
  2. mixed it until it mixed well
  3. put the dough into the pan
  4. put the pan on the stove an turn it on
  5. stir it until it boiled
  6. when its half boiled put 2 egg yolks and stir it agan until the dough was boiling
  7. after the dough was boiled turn off the stove and pour the dough into the pudding mold
  8. wait until it cold and put in in refrigerator for 4 hours
  9. after 4 hours in refrigerator put in on the tray
  10. .(optional) spread chocolatos iitalian chocolate
  11. cut it into a slices

AND FINNALY THE CHOCOLATE BREAD PUDDING IS READY TO SERVE
